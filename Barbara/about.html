About Astute Medical Legal Nurse Consulting, LLC:

Astute Medical Legal Nurse Consulting is an independent full service company, providing consulting services to attorneys, insurance companies, and organizations.

Offices located in New Jersey and Texas, services offered nationally.


Founder Barbara Schweiger Albanese, MSN, RN, CCRN, CLNC

Barbara has over 30 years of experience in health care. Her acute care experience includes critical care, cardiac, trauma and neurosurgery, education, and nursing administration.

She holds an adjunct nursing faculty position and maintains instructor status for Advanced Life Support and Trauma Nursing. She has held national certification in Critical Care Nursing for over 20+ years.

She has served as a working member on multiple committees focusing on quality, safety, and risk management.

She actively participated in the creation and implementation of electronic documentation screens for nursing documentation.
