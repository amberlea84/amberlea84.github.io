About

I'm originally from Northern California. My wanderlust has taken me all over the country, and five years ago I landed in South Philly. I currently live just a few miles away in Collingswood and I love it! It's small town America with all the benefits of the city.

When I'm not traveling, I'm obsessing over web development. My goal is to become a full-stack developer, but currently the bulk of my knowledge lies in front-end development.
